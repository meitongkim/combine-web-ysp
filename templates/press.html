Boilerplate. Youth for Sustainable Policy (YSP) is a youth‑led, non‑profit policy initiative based in Singapore. We run six asynchronous policy routes for young people aged 16–28 across the region — Research, Policy, Business, Diplomacy, Environment, and Community. The work is free, mentor‑matched, and ends in a published capstone under the fellow's byline.
