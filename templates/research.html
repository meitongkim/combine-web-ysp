After the heat dome — adaptive cooling pilots in three mid‑size cities.

Da Nang, Modesto, and Faisalabad each ran a different version of a public‑cool‑space pilot in the summer of 2025. A YSP fellow embedded with each city's planning office for six weeks. This brief is what they found — and what didn't translate.

The Library Letter · Monthly

A short, careful email about the research that landed.

One Sunday a month. Three new briefs, one author interview, and a quiet update on the work nobody else covers. Written by the fellows themselves — no engagement bait, no tracking pixels.

4,200+ readers across 38 countries — including policymakers, librarians, journalists, and a surprising number of fellow fellows. One email a month. Unsubscribe in one click. We don't share your address.
