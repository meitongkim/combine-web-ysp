YSP is currently mid‑cycle. Our inaugural Annual Report — covering all activity in calendar year 2026 — will be compiled in the first three weeks of January 2027 and published in full here, free and open access, by 31 January 2027.

What the 2026 report will cover

Six chapters · ~40 pages
01 — Programmes

Cohorts & capstones

Intake numbers, completion rates, and a public index of every capstone shipped across the six membership routes.

02 — Research

Briefs & field notes

Every paper, brief, and pilot published under the YSP byline in 2026, with citations, partners, and policy uptake noted.

03 — Reach

Regions & readership

Member geographies, partner institutions, newsletter and reading‑room growth — all reported with raw numbers, not vanity metrics.

04 — Finance

Income & spend

Audited statement of income and expenditure, grant sources, and a fellow‑stipend breakdown. No revenue is undisclosed.

05 — Safeguarding

Reports & response

Aggregated and anonymised count of safeguarding reports received, response times, and outcomes — per our Content Safety Framework.

06 — Governance

Team & advisors

Board composition, conflicts of interest, senior advisor list, and a statement from the Director of Operations.
