Sync with Google Calendar

Subscribe to your personalized YSP Learns calendar feed. This dynamically syncs your module starts, target deadlines, and completions directly to Google Calendar, Apple Calendar, or Outlook in real-time.

Google Calendar subscription steps:

  1. Copy the secure link above.
  2. Open Google Calendar in your web browser.
  3. On the left panel, click the plus sign (+) next to Other calendars, then choose From URL.
  4. Paste the copied URL in the text field and click Add calendar.
  5. Google Calendar will now subscribe to your feed and update your events automatically!
