Privacy Notice

Youth for Sustainable Policy ("YSP", "we", "us") respects your privacy. This notice explains what minimal data we handle and why.

Effective 1 January 2026 Governed by Singapore PDPA
01

What we collect

When you use our contact or newsletter forms, you provide: your name, email address, and message. We do not collect payment information, location data, or any other personal data beyond what you voluntarily provide.

02

Why we collect it

Your name and email are used solely to respond to your enquiry or send you the newsletter you requested. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or third‑party data sharing.

03

How long we keep it

Contact form submissions are retained only as long as necessary to fulfil your enquiry and are deleted within 90 days. Newsletter subscribers may unsubscribe at any time.

04

No tracking

This website does not use third‑party analytics cookies, tracking pixels, or advertising scripts. We may use basic server‑level access logs for security purposes only.

05

Your rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time. Contact youthforsustainablepolicy@gmail.com to exercise these rights.

06

No account

YSP does not require account creation. We do not store passwords or persistent user profiles.

Questions about your data?

We answer every privacy enquiry personally, usually within 48 hours.
