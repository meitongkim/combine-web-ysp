How we hire

Slow journalism, fast trust.

No timed assessments, no whiteboarding. A short call, a paid trial brief, and a decision within two weeks — the same care we bring to the policy work itself.

01 — Application

A letter, not a form

Tell us about the policy problem you can't stop thinking about. Two paragraphs is plenty; CV optional.

02 — Conversation

One 30‑minute call

With the hiring lead. No panel rounds, no behavioural drills. Bring questions.

03 — Paid trial

A real brief, shipped

Five days, paid at our standard rate, mentored end‑to‑end. You keep the byline either way.
