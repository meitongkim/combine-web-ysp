Community Policy

YSP is committed to being a safe, respectful, and inclusive space for all youth aged 16–28.

By engaging with YSP — whether submitting research, participating in mentorship, or using this platform — you agree to the following.
01

Respectful communication

All interactions must be respectful. Harassment, discrimination, or hostile conduct based on race, gender, nationality, religion, disability, or any other characteristic is prohibited and will result in removal.

02

Accurate representation

Do not misrepresent your credentials, age, affiliation, or the content of your research. Plagiarism or submission of work that is not your own is a violation of this policy.

03

No harmful content

Content that promotes violence, hate speech, self‑harm, illegal activities, or personal attacks against named individuals is strictly prohibited.

04

Youth protection

YSP takes the safety of young members (ages 16–18) especially seriously. Any adult engaging with minors through the platform must do so within the bounds of YSP's safeguarding guidelines. Report concerns immediately to youthforsustainablepolicy@gmail.com.

05

No account required

YSP does not require you to create an account. No personal data is retained beyond what is strictly necessary to fulfil your enquiry.

06

Violations & consequences

Violations may result in removal from YSP programmes, cancellation of mentorship arrangements, and — where legally required — reporting to relevant authorities.

Need to report something?

We treat every safeguarding report seriously, confidentially, and quickly.
