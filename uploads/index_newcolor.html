Sixteen Weeks. One Capstone. Your Name on It.

Six membership routes. Four intake seasons per year. Fully asynchronous, mentor-matched, and free — designed for youth 16–28 who want to produce original policy work under their own byline.

Research Route

01

Step One

Find Your Policy Niche

We help you turn your existing interests, lived experiences, and community context into a focused, credible policy research angle. The more specific, the more powerful.

  • Identify a clear policy focus rooted in your real context
  • Connect past experiences into one compelling research strand
  • Avoid generic topics by leaning into niche, local impact

"The niche isn't limiting — it's liberating. Specific always wins over general."

02

Step Two

Launch Your Initiative

This is where most young researchers stall. YSP helps you move from idea to published output — a policy brief, research report, community dialogue, or media piece.

  • Step-by-step guidance from research question to publication
  • Use YSP's research hub to find data, collaborators, and mentors
  • Publish, recruit, and get your initiative off the ground

"Most students wait until everything is perfect. YSP helps you publish while learning."

03

Step Three

Scale Your Impact

Sustainable policy institutions care about depth, growth, and results. YSP helps you scale what you've launched into something that lasts beyond you.

  • Focus on quantifiable outcomes: reach, citations, policy uptake
  • Strengthen your research with cross-border partnerships
  • Expand your project strategically, not randomly

"One initiative with measurable reach will outlast ten with none."

Community Policy

Youth for Sustainable Policy is committed to being a safe, respectful, and inclusive space for all youth aged 16–28. By engaging with YSP — whether submitting research, participating in mentorship, or using this platform — you agree to the following.

Respectful Communication. All interactions must be respectful. Harassment, discrimination, or hostile conduct based on race, gender, nationality, religion, disability, or any other characteristic is prohibited and will result in removal.

Accurate Representation. Do not misrepresent your credentials, age, affiliation, or the content of your research. Plagiarism or submission of work that is not your own is a violation of this policy.

No Harmful Content. Content that promotes violence, hate speech, self-harm, illegal activities, or personal attacks against named individuals is strictly prohibited.

Youth Protection. YSP takes the safety of young members (ages 16–18) especially seriously. Any adult engaging with minors through the platform must do so within the bounds of YSP's safeguarding guidelines. Report concerns immediately to youthforsustainablepolicy@gmail.com.

No Account Required. YSP does not require you to create an account. No personal data is retained beyond what is strictly necessary to fulfil your enquiry.

Violations may result in removal from YSP programmes, cancellation of mentorship arrangements, and — where legally required — reporting to relevant authorities.

Privacy Notice

Youth for Sustainable Policy ("YSP", "we", "us") respects your privacy. This notice explains what minimal data we handle and why.

What We Collect. When you use our contact or newsletter forms, you provide: your name, email address, and message. We do not collect payment information, location data, or any other personal data beyond what you voluntarily provide.

Why We Collect It. Your name and email are used solely to respond to your enquiry or send you the newsletter you requested. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or third-party data sharing.

How Long We Keep It. Contact form submissions are retained only as long as necessary to fulfil your enquiry and are deleted within 90 days. Newsletter subscribers may unsubscribe at any time.

No Tracking. This website does not use third-party analytics cookies, tracking pixels, or advertising scripts. We may use basic server-level access logs for security purposes only.

Your Rights. You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time. Contact youthforsustainablepolicy@gmail.com to exercise these rights.

No Account. YSP does not require account creation. We do not store passwords or persistent user profiles.

This notice is effective as of 1 January 2026 and is governed by Singapore's Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA).

Content Safety Framework

YSP maintains a multi-layer content safety framework to protect all members, with heightened protections for members under 18.

  • Human Review: All research submissions and project applications are reviewed by a YSP team member before publication or matching.
  • Front-End Protection: Forms include honeypot fields, client-side rate limiting, and input sanitisation to prevent spam and automated abuse.
  • No Anonymous Amplification: Content submitted to YSP is attributed and reviewed — there is no anonymous public posting feature.
  • Safeguarding Protocol: Mentor-member relationships are subject to YSP's safeguarding guidelines. All mentors undergo a light-touch vetting process.
  • Reporting: Any safety concern can be reported to youthforsustainablepolicy@gmail.com. We commit to acknowledging all reports within 24 hours.
  • No Sign-In: Because we do not collect account credentials, there is no risk of account compromise or password data breaches.

YSP follows Singapore's guidelines for youth-serving organisations and aligns with international best practices for youth digital safety.
